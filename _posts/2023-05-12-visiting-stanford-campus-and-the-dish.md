I visited Stanford campus and the Stanford Dish area. This visit was special for me because Stanford is connected in my mind with machine learning, deep learning, and some of the courses that helped me in my work.

<!-- more -->

Before joining LandingAI, I took Andrew Ng's Deep Learning and MLOps courses on Coursera. Later, I worked at LandingAI, where Andrew Ng is the founder and chairman. Because of that, visiting Stanford felt connected to my own path as a software engineer.

I also hiked around the Stanford Dish area. The hike gave me a different view of the place: open hills, wide paths, and views back toward the campus and the Bay Area. It was quiet enough to think, and it made the visit feel more complete.

{% image /assets/images/2023-05-12-stanford-campus-and-the-dish/PXL_20230513_001125454.MP.webp 1125 1500 %}

Looking back, I liked this visit because it connected study, work, and place. I had learned from Stanford-related courses before, and being there in person made that history feel closer.

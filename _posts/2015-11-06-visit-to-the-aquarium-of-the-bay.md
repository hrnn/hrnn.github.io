description: A visit to Aquarium of the Bay in San Francisco, seeing sea life up close as someone from Bolivia, a landlocked country.

I visited the Aquarium of the Bay in San Francisco, and it was one of the places I enjoyed the most during the trip.

For people from Bolivia, it is not common to see sea life so close. We do not have access to the sea, so watching fish, jellyfish, and other animals under the water felt very special to me.

I stayed there for a long time. The pictures tell most of the story.

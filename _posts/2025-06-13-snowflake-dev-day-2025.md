In June 2025, I went to Snowflake Dev Day at Moscone Center in San Francisco. The event was part of Snowflake Summit 2025, and it focused on developers building with data, AI, and applications.

{% image /assets/images/2025-06-13-snowflake-dev-day-2025/IMG_1582.webp 1500 538 %}

<!-- more -->

The day had technical talks, demos, community spaces, and hands-on content around Snowflake's AI Data Cloud. I was interested in how teams are thinking about agents, app development, machine learning, and modern data workflows.

{% image /assets/images/2025-06-13-snowflake-dev-day-2025/IMG_1513.webp 1500 1125 %}

What stood out to me was the mix of conference energy and practical developer content. It was not only about announcements. It was also a place to see ideas that developers can try in real projects.
